Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of sleeping with a wedge pillow for acid reflux?
Sleeping with a wedge pillow can help reduce nighttime heartburn and GERD symptoms by raising the head and torso to keep stomach acid from flowing back up into the esophagus.
How do you choose the right size wedge pillow for sleeping upright?
The size of the wedge should be based on your body size and sleeping position. Generally, taller people will need larger wedges while shorter people can use smaller wedges.
Is it safe to sleep on a wedge pillow every night?
Yes, as long as it is comfortable and supportive, it is generally safe to sleep on a wedge pillow every night if needed to manage acid reflux symptoms.
Are there any side effects associated with using a bed wedge for sleeping upright?
In some cases, sleeping in an elevated position can lead to neck or back pain if not done correctly, so proper posture is important when using a bed wedge while sleeping.
