What is a medical wedge pillow?
A medical wedge pillow is a specialized type of pillow designed to provide support and comfort while sleeping in an upright position.

Benefits of using a medical wedge pillow include improved breathing, reduced acid reflux, improved digestion, and relief from neck and back pain.

The size of your medical wedge pillow should depend on your body size, sleeping position, and mattress thickness. Generally, you should choose a pocket depth between 4-7 inches.
